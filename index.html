More benefits from our product

Deep Moisture and Protein Protection

Coconut oil is one of the few oils rich in lauric acid, which has a low molecular weight. This allows it to penetrate deep into the hair shaft rather than just sitting on the surface. It helps reduce protein loss for both undamaged and damaged hair, making it an excellent pre-wash treatment.

Scalp Health and Dandruff Control

The traditional extraction method ensures that the oil's antimicrobial and antifungal properties remain intact. Applying it to the scalp can help soothe dryness and combat the fungus associated with dandruff. Its cooling nature is also traditionally used to reduce "body heat," which is often linked to scalp irritation.

Strengthening and Damage Repair

Because it is cold-pressed, the oil retains its high antioxidant content (such as Vitamin E). These antioxidants shield the hair from environmental damage, pollution, and UV rays. Regular use can lead to fewer split ends and reduced hair breakage.

Natural Shine and Texture

Unlike refined oils that may contain hexane or bleaching agents, wood-pressed oil maintains a natural viscosity and aroma. It smooths the hair cuticle, which helps the hair reflect light, resulting in a natural, healthy sheen without the "sticky" feeling of synthetic oils.

Traditional Application Tips

The Warmth Method: Instead of heating the oil directly on a flame (which can degrade it), place a small amount in a bowl and set that bowl inside a larger container of warm water.

Scalp Massage: Traditionally, the oil is applied using the fingertips in circular motions to improve blood circulation to the follicles.

The Pre-Wash Ritual: Apply the oil at least 30 minutes before washing. For deeper conditioning, leave it in overnight to allow maximum penetration.
